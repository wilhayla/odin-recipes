Pesto Pasta

Anyone can make a Pesto Pasta, but not everyone knows how to make
a pesto pasta that’s slick with plenty of pesto sauce without adding tons of
extra oil! Here’s how I make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 ½ tablespoons pesto or more to taste
  • salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2

    Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta
    and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet
    firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.

  3. Step 3

    SMeanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and
    stir until softened, about 3 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.

  5. Step 5

    Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
